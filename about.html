This prototype is part of a master's research at Keio University - Systen Design and Management

The prototype was built in order to explore, if an AI assistant can help to improve opreational efficiency of medical representatives in Japan.

Prototype Architecture:

The prototype is hosted on AWS, using Amplify. The code is devided in three steps which are executed by three AWS Lambda-functions. Metadata of papers, such as AI generation of summaries and translations are handled via APIs.
